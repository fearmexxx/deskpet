DESKPET MVP FEATURES

Version 1.0 (Tactical Neon Build) • Status: Operational

🔌 EXTENSION INFRASTRUCTURE

  • Manifest V3 Architecture: Secure, performant service worker core utilizing chrome APIs.
  • State Management: Persistent offline data sync using Chrome Storage and IndexedDB.
  • Passive Time Tracker: Processes catch-up ticks on tab load or alarms, rewarding XP and consuming energy periodically.

🎨 PROCEDURAL RENDER ENGINE

  • Cyber-Neon Glow: Utilizes canvas shadow blur coordinates to draw pixel-art assets with dynamic glowing boundaries.
  • 5 Evolution Stages: Egg (Lv. 1-10) ➔ Baby (Lv. 11-30) ➔ Juvenile (Lv. 31-60) ➔ Adult (Lv. 61-90) ➔ Legendary (Lv. 91-100).
  • Particle Physics: Triggers Zzz bubbles for sleeping, hearts/diamonds for excited, and rain drops for low happiness.

🛸 WEBPAGE FLOATING OVERLAY

  • Draggable Overlay: Mouse drag-and-drop mechanics with screen boundary collision checks.
  • Hover HUD Tooltip: Displays pet name, level badges, XP percentages, energy, and happiness status.
  • Gapless Actions Menu: In-context quick actions (Pet, Feed, HUD) with a seamless, zero-gap hover transition to prevent disappearing.

📱 DROPDOWN POPUP

  • Compact HUD: View pet status, level, name, and XP bar immediately on clicking the extension icon.
  • Sleep Toggle: Toggle sleeping status to freeze/unfreeze animations and Zzz particle streams.
  • Quick Feed/Pet: Boost happiness and energy stats right from the browser toolbar dropdown.

🎮 STATION DASHBOARD & MINI-GAMES

• Pet Arena & Customization:

A large room where the pet wanders. Custom rename inputs and 4 glowing neon color skins (Cyan, Pink, Green, Gold).

• Grid Memory Match:

Find matching cards in a 16-card grid with 3D flip card animations to claim +20 XP and +10 $DESK.

• Neon Runner:

Side-scrolling infinite canvas jumper. Press spacebar or click to jump spikes. Reach 150 score to earn +30 XP and +15 $DESK.

• Daily Bounty Board:

Tracks four daily tasks: keep active, play games, feed pet, and share progress. Claims rewards dynamically.

⛓️ SOLANA WEB3 SANDBOX & ECONOMY

• Phantom Wallet Simulation:

Interactive "Connect Wallet" flow, unlocking address display and token dashboards.

• Metaplex Core NFT Mint:

Unlockable at Level 10. Mint pet to the blockchain, simulating dynamic metadata updating, devnet transaction hashes, and status upgrades.

• SPL Token-2022 Yield:

Real-time token yield accumulation ticking on-screen. Base rate is boosted by pet level, attributes, and rarity skins.

• Genetic Breeding Lab:

Allows breeding two adult pets to spawn a rare egg, consuming/burning 100 $DESK tokens in the process.
